# CIVE Cafeteria Management System - System Scope

## 1. System Scope (Wigo wa Mfumo)

### 1.1 Introduction (Utangulizi)
The CIVE Cafeteria Management System is a web-based application designed to streamline food ordering and management processes at the College of Informatics and Virtual Education (CIVE), University of Dodoma (UDOM).

Mfumo wa Usimamizi wa Cafeteria ya CIVE ni programu ya mtandao iliyoundwa kurahisisha michakato ya kuagiza na kusimamia chakula katika Chuo cha Taarifa na Elimu Mtandao (CIVE), Chuo Kikuu cha Dodoma (UDOM).

### 1.2 Problem Statement (Kauli ya Tatizo)
**Current Challenges (Changamoto za Sasa):**
- Long queues during peak hours (Foleni ndefu wakati wa saa za kuhudumiwa)
- Students missing classes due to waiting time (Wanafunzi kukosa masomo kwa muda wa kungoja)
- Manual order processing leading to errors (Shughuli za maagizo za kufanya kwa mikono zinaleta makosa)
- Food wastage due to poor demand forecasting (Upotevu wa chakula kutokana na utabiri dhaifu wa mahitaji)
- No real-time stock tracking (Hakuna ufuatiliaji wa stock kwa wakati halisi)
- Limited payment options (Chaguo chache za malipo)

### 1.3 Objectives (Malengo)

#### Primary Objectives (Malengo Kuu):
1. Reduce waiting time by 60% through online pre-ordering (Punguza muda wa kungoja kwa 60% kupitia kuagiza mtandaoni)
2. Eliminate manual order shouting (Aondoe kelele za kuagiza kwa sauti)
3. Provide real-time stock visibility (Toa uonekano wa stock kwa wakati halisi)
4. Enable mobile money payments (Wezesha malipo kwa simu)

#### Secondary Objectives (Malengo ya Pili):
1. Generate sales reports for management (Tengeneza ripoti za mauzo kwa usimamizi)
2. Track food popularity and consumption patterns (Fuatilia utrendi wa chakula na mifumo ya matumizi)
3. Provide feedback mechanism for continuous improvement (Toa njia ya maoni kwa uboreshaji endelevu)

### 1.4 Scope Boundaries (Mipaka ya Wigo)

#### In Scope (Ndani ya Wigo):
✅ Online food ordering for students (Kuagiza chakula mtandaoni kwa wanafunzi)
✅ Menu management with stock status (Usimamizi wa menyu na hali ya stock)
✅ Order processing by cashiers (Shughuli za maagizo na wahudumu wa fedha)
✅ Kitchen order queue management (Usimamizi wa foleni ya maagizo jikoni)
✅ Mobile payment integration (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money)
✅ Sales reporting and analytics (Ripoti za mauzo na uchanganuzi)
✅ Feedback collection (Ukusanyaji wa maoni)
✅ Real-time waiting time estimation (Utabiri wa muda wa kungoja kwa wakati halisi)

#### Out of Scope (Nje ya Wigo):
❌ Delivery service (Huduma ya kusafirisha)
❌ Table reservation (Kuhifadhi meza)
❌ Inventory management for suppliers (Usimamizi wa stock kwa wasambazaji)
❌ Integration with university student ID system (Kuunganishwa na mfumo wa ID ya wanafunzi wa chuo)
❌ Multi-campus support (Usaidizi wa maeneo mbalimbali ya chuo)

### 1.5 Stakeholders (Wadau)

| Stakeholder | Role | Interest/Concern |
|-------------|------|------------------|
| Students | Primary Users | Fast service, accurate orders, mobile payments |
| Cafeteria Manager | Administrator | Sales reports, stock management, efficiency |
| Cashiers | Staff | Easy order processing, payment tracking |
| Cooks/Kitchen Staff | Staff | Clear order queue, portion control |
| University Administration | Overseer | Revenue tracking, student satisfaction |

### 1.6 Success Criteria (Vigezo vya Mafanikio)

1. **Performance:** Average order processing time < 3 minutes (Utendaji: Muda wastani wa shughuli za maagizo < dakika 3)
2. **Adoption:** 70% of students use the system within 3 months (Ufikiaji: Wanafunzi 70% watumia mfumo ndani ya miezi 3)
3. **Accuracy:** Order error rate < 2% (Usahihi: Kiwango cha makosa cha maagizo < 2%)
4. **Availability:** System uptime > 99% (Upatikanaji: Mfumo uko hai > 99%)
5. **Satisfaction:** Customer satisfaction rating > 4.0/5.0 (Kuridhika: Kiwango cha kuridhika kwa wateja > 4.0/5.0)

### 1.7 Constraints (Vikwazo)

1. **Technical:** Must work on low-bandwidth mobile networks (Kiufundi: Lazima ifanye kazi kwenye mitandao ya simu yenye bandwidth ndogo)
2. **Budget:** Limited development and maintenance budget (Bajeti: Bajeti ndogo ya uundaji na utunzaji)
3. **Time:** Must be deployed before next semester (Muda: Lazima imewekwa kabla ya semester ijayo)
4. **Security:** Must comply with university data protection policies (Usalama: Lazima ilinde sera za ulinzi wa data za chuo)
